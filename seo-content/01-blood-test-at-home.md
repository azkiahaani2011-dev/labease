## What Is a Home Blood Test?

A home blood test (also called home sample collection) is a service where a certified phlebotomist visits your home or office to collect a blood, urine, or other biological sample. The sample is then taken to an NABL-accredited laboratory for analysis, and results are shared via WhatsApp, email, or the LabEase app.

You get the same accuracy as a lab visit — with zero travel hassle.

## How to Prepare for a Home Blood Test

**For fasting tests (Blood Sugar, Lipid Profile, LFT, KFT):**
- Fast for **8–12 hours** before the test
- Drink plain water — it helps the phlebotomist find your vein
- Avoid tea, coffee, juice, or any food

**For non-fasting tests (CBC, Thyroid, Dengue, Vitamin D):**
- No special preparation required
- Wear a loose-sleeved shirt for easy access

**General tips:**
- Stay hydrated
- Inform the phlebotomist of any medications you take
- Keep your booking confirmation ready
